Digital education

() {

return The main track of this edition will be "Digital Education: captivity factory or new emancipation?". This central track will be present throughout the festival. It is therefore not exclusive, but we will give priority to proposals for formats related to it. The question of digital education will make it possible to address the major challenges of education through the use of these technologies. This theme covers education from primary to higher education, including associative, amateur and continuous learning, while questioning their societal implications in general.

}
