Meeting - "creative practices in free music"

Within the framework of the RMLL Hackstub & Longevity propose a round table around the practices of creation in free music. What is free music? Free music, like free software, is usually defined as music that can be freely copied, distributed and modified. Thus, free music is either music from the public domain or music broadcast under a Free License. It thus gathers all musical genres and could well have a very particular affinity with electronic music and hip-hop by their practices: remix, sampling, rap, and so on.
